Description
Ayham from Grade 11 has developed an Android app. The application, CamCurrency, utilizes the user's phone camera in order to scan the prices displayed in a shop located in a country foreign to the user. Then, the prices are converted to the user's native currency. In his presentation, Ayham demontrated the usage of his application. He has also presented the difficulties he overcame when developing his application, discussing the tools he used and limitations of his software. Amongst other things, he discussed broader topics such as the common notion for students to claim programming as refurbishing online code. Teachers and students were appreciative of his work, wishing him goodluck towards his endeavor of publishing his app on the Google Play Store platform.
