PROJECTS
Description
Under the pursuit of our vision and mission statement, and our endeavours to provide our students every support, JIS (Jubail International School) High School Committee organized a career counselling session with our Alumni for the students of grades 11 and 12. The rationale of the session was to keep our students updated with admission criteria, the latest admission policies, admission programmes, prospects, and other opportunities such as scholarship programs etc. Our presenter Omar Khan, from Australia, brought many pragmatic aspects to the limelight which motivated our students to plan their future careers in the best feasible way. The session was also followed by questions from the audience which showed their enthusiasm in the process. Overall, not only was it an honor to see our Alumni live through digital technology but also a privilege to see our present students buckling up for future challenges. Although the session ended in its due time; i.e., 1 hour 30, the thirst of students seemed unquenchable.
